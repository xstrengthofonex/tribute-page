About

  • Robert Cecil Martin (colloquially known as Uncle Bob) is an American software engineer and author.
  • He is a co-author of the Agile Manifesto.
  • He now runs a consulting firm called Uncle Bob Consulting LLC and Clean Coders which hosts videos based on his experiences and books.
  • He elaborated important concepts: SOLID principles, Transformation Priority Premise in TDD, Clean Architecture and Software Craftsmanship
